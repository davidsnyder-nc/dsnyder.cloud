Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 26, 2025

No Advertisements or Tracking

This website does not use advertisements of any kind. We do not use tracking technologies or analytics scripts.

Information We Collect

Account registration does not require email verification. The only information collected is your email address and password, which are used solely for authentication purposes to allow you to log in to your account. This information is not used for marketing or any other purpose.

Your Data

Your saved recipes, recipe history, and dietary preferences are stored securely in your account. This data is only accessible to you when you are logged in.

Cookies

A single session cookie may be used to keep you logged in during your visit. This cookie is essential for the site's functionality and is not used for tracking or advertising.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our application:

  • Google Firebase: Used for user authentication and database storage. You can review their privacy policy here: Firebase Privacy & Security.
  • Google Gemini AI: Used for generating recipes and meal plans. Data is processed by Google's services. You can review their privacy policy here: Google Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any payment information or location data.

Data Control and Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can view, edit, export, or delete your saved recipes and plans at any time through your account. You may also delete your entire account, which will permanently remove all of your stored data from our systems.

Security

We take your privacy seriously. Passwords are never stored in plain text and are securely hashed.

Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with a new effective date.
